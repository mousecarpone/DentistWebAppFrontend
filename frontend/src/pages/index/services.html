Our Services

At San Diego Dental Studio, we offer a comprehensive range of dental services to meet all your oral health needs.

General Dentistry

Dental Exams & Cleanings

Regular check-ups and professional cleanings to maintain optimal oral health.

Dental X-Rays

Digital x-rays to detect issues not visible during a regular dental exam.

Fillings

Tooth-colored fillings to restore teeth affected by decay.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments for a brighter, more confident smile.

Veneers

Custom-made shells to cover the front surface of teeth to improve appearance.

Invisalign

Clear aligners to straighten teeth without traditional braces.

Restorative Dentistry

Crowns & Bridges

Restore damaged teeth and replace missing teeth with natural-looking prosthetics.

Dental Implants

Permanent replacement for missing teeth that look and function like natural teeth.

Dentures

Full and partial dentures to replace multiple missing teeth.
